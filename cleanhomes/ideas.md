## Selected Approach: **Approach A — Nordic Spa Minimalism**

Chosen for its perfect alignment with the Apple-like minimalist brief: warm, premium, spacious, and deeply trustworthy. The DM Serif Display + Inter pairing creates editorial sophistication without being cold. The forest green + warm sand palette is distinctive, original, and signals both cleanliness and premium quality — entirely different from cleanwhale.pl's blue/yellow palette.
